    EDYC Sail Training Summer 20

    Posted on June 26, 2020 Categories: Information, Training Comments are off for this post

    Sail Training at EDYC in the summer of 2020 will be taking a different format, which is being developed and planned to meet all RYA and Govt Covid-19 requirements., thus it is in an advancing state.

    We know there is demand there and that we have instructors willing to provide the training.

    This year we need to change the way in which we provide training for young people and adults. Sadly, we need to cancel this year’s planned Youth Week. Instead, we can offer more opportunities for shorter periods but with restrictions on the numbers we can take, and the minimum competence needed to take part. 

    We have the plans in an advanced state and will be releasing them soon subject to various compliance checks. Please bear with us as these final stages are completed and be prepared to work with us in terms of training delivery.

    Email to members – will be one method of information delivery plus posts on this club web site.
